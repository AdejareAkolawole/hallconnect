2018 Story

HCMC LTD began operation under section 6 of the cooperative societies law chapter 14 cooperative law 2015 on July 27th, 2018, with just 11 cooperators.

HCMC LTD commenced operations on July 27,2018 under Section 6 of the Cooperative Societies Law, Chapter 14 (2015). The Cooperative started with a foundationn of just 11 memebers, embodying the principles of mutual support, finanacial empowerment, and collective growth. Since it's inception, HCMC LTD has remained commited to fostering economic development and financial inclusion among it's memebers. leveraging cooperative principles to achieve sustainable prosperity.

The 1st Annual General Meeting (AGM) was held on Febuary 18, 2019, marking a significant milestone in the cooperative's journey. During the AGM, members expressed satisfaction with the achievements recorded within the first year of operations. Key successes included steady financial growth, increased meembership, and effective management of resources , reinforcing confidence in the cooperative's future.
