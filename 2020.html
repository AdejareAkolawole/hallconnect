2020 Story

HCMC LTD which commenced operations on July 27, 2018, under Section 6 of the Cooperative Societies Law, Chapter 14 (2015), with just 11 founding members, and was established to promote financial empowerment, economic growth, and mutual support among its members.

At the 1st Annual General Meeting (AGM) was held on February 18, 2019, members expressed satisfaction with the cooperative’s early successes, and by December 2019, membership had expanded to 22 cooperators, and the cooperative recorded a profit of N25,000.

During the 2nd AGM, held on March 17, 2020, at Hall Connects Center, members reflected on the progress in membership and financial strength. The leadership encouraged members to remain committed to the cooperative’s vision for growth and sustainability.

By December 2020, HCMC LTD had grown to 31 members, reflecting its steady acceptance and appeal among cooperators. The financial outlook also saw a remarkable improvement, with a profit of N47,000 recorded in the cooperative’s balance sheet.

A major milestone was achieved during this period: for the first time in the cooperative’s history, dividends were distributed to shareholders. A dividend payout of 4 kobo per unit of N100.00 was approved, marking a significant achievement in rewarding members' investment and confidence in the cooperative. The 3rd AGM, held at Connects Centre, was a momentous occasion, as members celebrated this financial milestone and looked forward to continued success.

Chairman’s Speech

The Chairman, in his address, commended the cooperative’s resilience, strategic management, and the unwavering commitment of members. He highlighted the significance of achieving profitability and issuing dividends within such a short span, describing it as a testament to the cooperative’s sound financial structure and forward-thinking approach. He further encouraged members to increase their participation, emphasizing that the more investments and contributions made, the greater the dividends and benefits for everyone. He assured members that with continued discipline, accountability, and unity, HCMC LTD would achieve even greater financial success.

General Secretary’s Speech

The General Secretary provided a detailed report on the cooperative’s financial performance, outlining revenue growth, membership expansion, and strategic investments made during the financial year. He highlighted how the cooperative had overcome economic challenges to achieve profitability and distribute dividends for the first time.

He also emphasized the importance of financial literacy, commitment, and transparency in ensuring the cooperative’s continued success. He encouraged members to take advantage of available opportunities to grow their investments and actively participate in decision-making processes.The meeting concluded with a vote of confidence in the executive leadership, and members expressed optimism for greater dividends and increased profitability in the coming years.
