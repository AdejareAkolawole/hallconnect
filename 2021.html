HALL CONNECTS (LEKKI) MULTI-PURPOSE COOPERATIVE SOCIETY LTD. Is unlike other industry associations or networking platforms you might know, because it is the only place where values-led organizations from across sectors ( Health, manufacturing, Production, Service, Finance, Entertainment etc) come together to strengthen their capacities in a non-competitive environment. Hcmc 65 cooperators came together and registered with Old Mutual Insurance under the Health Insurance scheme.

2021- Performance and Achievements

HCMC LTD continued its trajectory of growth and financial success in 2021, marking another milestone in the cooperative’s journey. By the end of the year, the cooperative’s membership had grown to 65 cooperators, reflecting increasing trust and participation.

A significant achievement during the year was the registration of HCMC LTD and its members with Old Mutual Insurance under a Health Insurance Scheme. This strategic move was aimed at providing members with improved healthcare access, ensuring their well-being, and strengthening the cooperative’s welfare initiatives.

Financially, the cooperative recorded impressive results. Shares, dividends, and bonuses were issued to members, reinforcing the commitment to rewarding investment and participation. A special bonus of 1 share for every 5 shares held was granted, encouraging further investment and boosting member confidence in the cooperative’s financial stability.

The 4th Annual General Meeting (AGM) was held on April 22, 2022, at the Hall Connect Office. It was a highly anticipated gathering, as members celebrated the cooperative’s achievements and looked forward to greater opportunities.

Chairman’s Speech

The Chairman expressed gratitude to all members for their unwavering support and commitment. He emphasized the importance of the health insurance registration, noting that it was a significant step towards ensuring members' well-being and financial security.

He also highlighted the increase in membership and the cooperative’s ability to sustain profitability while issuing dividends and share bonuses. The Chairman reiterated that the cooperative's strength lay in collective effort and prudent financial management, urging members to continue their active participation and investment.

General Secretary’s Speech

The General Secretary provided an overview of the financial performance and strategic initiatives undertaken during the year. He commended members for their confidence in the cooperative, which had led to steady growth.

He detailed how the issuance of dividends and share bonuses was a reflection of the cooperative’s sound financial health. He encouraged members to take advantage of the share bonus policy, as it presented a great opportunity for long-term gains.

Furthermore, he noted that the partnership with Old Mutual Insurance was a landmark achievement, ensuring that members had access to quality healthcare, which in turn would enhance productivity and financial stability.

The meeting ended with a renewed commitment from members to expand the cooperative’s financial base, increase investments, and explore more welfare initiatives that would benefit all members in the long run.
