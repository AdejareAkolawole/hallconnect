2022 COOPERATIVE ECOSYSTEM: "One for all and all for one"

The 2022 Annual General Meeting (AGM) of HCMC LTD was a landmark event, reflecting the cooperative’s continued expansion and success. Held on May 3, 2023, at Exclusive Mansion, Oniru, Lekki, the meeting was themed:“COOPERATIVE ECOSYSTEM: One for All and All for One”

This theme underscored the spirit of unity, shared growth, and collective financial empowerment that had driven HCMC LTD forward. By the end of 2022, the cooperative had recorded significant achievements, including:

1. Membership growth to over 120 members from diverse economic backgrounds.

2. Increased dividends, reflecting improved financial performance.

3. Greater inclusivity, as professionals, entrepreneurs, and artisans joined the cooperative, strengthening its economic base.

President's Remark

The Chairman commended members for their dedication and trust in the cooperative model, emphasizing how HCMC LTD had grown into a thriving financial ecosystem. He celebrated the record membership growth and dividend increase, urging members to remain committed to the cooperative's vision.

He stressed that unity and collaboration remained key pillars of success and encouraged members to take full advantage of the financial and welfare opportunities within the cooperative.

General Secretary’s Speech

The General Secretary provided a detailed financial and membership report, noting that the cooperative’s ability to expand and sustain dividend payments was a testament to its strong management and collective participation.

He highlighted the importance of leveraging the diverse skills and industries within the cooperative, encouraging members to engage in business collaborations to further strengthen the cooperative’s financial ecosystem.

The meeting concluded on a high note, with members reaffirming their commitment to collective growth, financial security, and expanding opportunities within HCMC LTD.
