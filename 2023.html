HCMC SUCCESS STORIES : "Digital Economy"

As HCMC LTD continued its journey of growth and transformation, the adoption of digital cooperative services played a crucial role in enhancing efficiency and expanding opportunities for members. By integrating digital banking, marketing, and management tools, the cooperative improved its operations, making financial services more accessible and transparent for members.

Annual General Meeting (AGM) – June 6, 2024 The AGM was held at Exclusive Hotel, Lekki, on June 6, 2024, marking another milestone in the cooperative’s success story.

Key achievements from the past year included:

1.Membership growth to 167 members.

2. Total profit of N1.756,000 recorded

3. Dividend distribution of N185,000

4. Loans to members increased to N18 million.

5. Supporting business and personal growth

6. Introduction of digital services, enhancing financial transactions and cooperative management.

Chairman’s Remark

The Chairman commended members for their unwavering trust and participation in the cooperative. He highlighted that the digital revolution in cooperative management had significantly improved operations, enabling faster transactions, better financial management, and more transparency.

He emphasized that HCMC LTD was committed to leveraging technology to create financial inclusion, improve loan accessibility, and provide members with better investment opportunities. He urged members to take full advantage of the digital tools available, noting that a strong digital presence would position the cooperative for long-term sustainability and competitiveness.

General Secretary’s Speech

The General Secretary provided an in-depth analysis of the cooperative’s financial performance, highlighting the remarkable increase in profits, membership, and loan accessibility. He attributed much of this success to the seamless integration of digital services, which had reduced administrative bottlenecks and enhanced financial transactions.

He stressed the importance of continued innovation in the cooperative’s digital strategy, encouraging members to actively engage with the cooperative’s online banking platforms, digital marketing opportunities, and automated management tools to maximize their benefits.

The meeting ended with a sense of optimism, as members embraced the digital transformation and looked forward to greater financial empowerment and improved services in the coming years.
