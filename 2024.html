FLEXIBLE MEMBERSHIP MODEL: "Sustainability, Social Inclusion, and Digital Transformation"

The 2024 performance report of Hall Connects Multipurpose Cooperative Society (HCMC LTD) marks a significant milestone as we continue to embody our core principles of sustainability, social inclusion, and digital transformation

HCMC continued to evolve new concepts as we embraced adaptation to global economic trends, technological advancements, and social needs. Our behavior reflected a growing commitment to sustainability, inclusivity, and community empowerment.

The 4 Key behavioral traits that dominated our activities included in 2024 are:

1. Digital Transformation

2. Sustainability Focus

3. Social and Financial Inclusion

4. Flexible Membership Models

Hall connects cooperative society positioned themselves not only as economic entities but as key drivers of community development and sustainable growth.

Outstanding Performance in 2024 have surpassed the 2023 performance, reflecting resilience and strategic growth Key highlights include:

1. Dividend Declaration: A dividend of 75 kobo per unit share has been declared, rewarding our esteemed members for their continued trust and support.

2. Net Profit: We recorded a net profit of N489,000.00, a commendable improvement over the previous year, showcasing our prudent financial management and sustainable growth strategy.

3. Deposit Growth: Deposits increased by 35% compared to the 2023 position, indicating heightened member confidence and robust savings culture.

4. Membership Growth: Our cooperative continues to expand, with membership rising to 179 active members, a testament to our inclusivity and growing appeal.

Driving Factors Behind Our Success

1. Sustainability Initiatives: Our focus on sustainable financial practices ensured we maintained stability in the face of economic fluctuations. Investments in eco-friendly business operations and support for members' entrepreneurial ventures contributed to this achievement. Our eco-friendly business practices align with global environmental goals and Circular Economy: Recycling and waste reduction.

2. Social Inclusion: HCMC LTD remains committed to empowering all members, regardless of socio-economic status. We provided tailored financial solutions, capacity-building programs, and community support initiatives that fostered growth among marginalized groups.Hcmc Co-ops offered microfinance services to improve access to credit and insurance for small-scale entrepreneurs and farmers.Women, youth, and rural communities were prioritized in leadership roles and decision-making processes.

3. Digital Transformation: Leveraging technology played a pivotal role in our success. The implementation of digital financial platforms enhanced efficiency, transparency, and accessibility, making it easier for members to save, borrow, and participate in cooperative activities. Hcmc Co-ops adopted digital tools for management, member engagement, and financial transactions for transparency and efficiency.Our website ( www.hallconnectscoop.com) says it all.

Looking Ahead

As we build on the successes of 2024, our focus for the coming year will be to:

Further leverage digital tools to improve member engagement and financial inclusion.

Strengthen sustainability efforts through partnerships and eco-conscious investments.

Expand membership by creating value-driven programs that appeal to younger demographics.

I extend my heartfelt appreciation to our management team, board members, and every cooperative member for their dedication and contributions to our collective success. Together, we will continue to drive growth and build a cooperative society that stands as a beacon of progress and inclusivity.
