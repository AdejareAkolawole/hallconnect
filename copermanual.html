About Our Co-operative

HCMC LTD began operation under section 6 of the cooperative Societies law chapter 14 Cooperative Law 2015 on 27th July 2018 with just 11 cooperators. By December 2019 membership grew to 22 cooperators and a profit of N25k was achieved. In December 2020 our strength has grew to 31 cooperators and we recorded a feat of success at the 2nd AGM as a profit of N47k dotted our Balance sheet and for the first time a dividend of 4Kobo was distributed to shareholders per a unit of N1OO.00. December 2021 marked another successful year as membership grew to 41 and management proposed a dividend of 6kobo per shares in considerations of the N92k subject to approval at the General meeting. HALL CONNECTS (LEKKI) MULTI-PURPOSE COOPERATIVE SOCIETY LTD. Is unlike other industry associations or networking platforms you might know, because it is the only place where values-led organizations from across sectors ( Health, manufacturing, Production, Service, Finance, Entertainment etc) come together to strengthen their capacities in a non-competitive environment.

Membership Benefits

Members enjoy exclusive benefits including competitive interest rates, voting rights, profit sharing, and access to special investment opportunities.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of financial services including personal banking, business loans, investment opportunities, and cooperative funding solutions.

Banking Features

Access modern banking features including online banking, mobile app, secure transactions, and 24/7 customer support.
